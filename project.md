# The Emechetian Resources Project

## Mission

To provide the most comprehensive, openly accessible, and continuously updated research platform dedicated to the literature of Buchi Emecheta.

## What is The Emechetian Resources Project?

Emechetian Resources is an open digital humanities project dedicated to the creation and maintenance of a research platform for Buchi Emecheta studies. It aims to curate, organise, preserve, and make accessible scholarly resources related to Buchi Emecheta's literary works.

The project begins with a searchable bibliography of academic publications, and it is designed to expand over time to include additional research resources, such as datasets, thematic indexes, research guides, digital tools, and other materials that support Emechetian studies.

## Vision

To become the primary open-access reference platform for researchers, students, and educators working on Buchi Emecheta's literature worldwide.
